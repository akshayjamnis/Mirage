Emotion Guide

Understanding what your feelings mean

😌
Calm
Positive
›
A state of peace and relaxation. Your mind is at ease, stress levels are low and you feel centered and in control of your thoughts.
😄
Happy
Positive
›
A feeling of joy and contentment. You are experiencing positive emotions, high energy and a general sense of wellbeing and satisfaction.
😟
Anxious
Caution
›
A sense of worry or unease about something uncertain. Often involves racing thoughts, tension in the body and a feeling of dread or nervousness.
😤
Stressed
Caution
›
Feeling overwhelmed by demands or pressure. Stress can affect sleep, focus and mood. Try deep breathing or short breaks to reset.
😔
Sad
Low
›
Feeling down, heavy or hopeless. Sadness is a natural emotion but if persistent it may need attention. Talking to someone can help greatly.
😡
Angry
Low
›
Intense feelings of frustration or rage. Anger often signals that a boundary has been crossed. Pause before reacting and identify the root cause.
😴
Fatigued
Caution
›
Mental or physical exhaustion that goes beyond just being tired. May indicate burnout or poor sleep. Rest, hydration and light movement can help.
🤗
Grateful
Positive
›
Feeling thankful and appreciative of people or moments in your life. Gratitude is strongly linked to improved mental health and life satisfaction.
🧘

Tip: Journaling daily for just 5 minutes can significantly reduce anxiety and improve emotional clarity over time.
